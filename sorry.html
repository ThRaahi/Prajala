I'm Sorry

with all my heart 💕
💌
My dearest Mayalau,

I am sorry. When you cut that call yesterday, I was busy and at first I didn't even realise you had cut it. My phone didn't give me any vibration. When I finally saw it, I thought you were busy or on a call with someone else. That's why you cut it. Because normally, you don't.

And I'm not even sure if you were calling out to me while we were still on the call. Maybe I didn't hear you. Maybe I missed it completely. And I am so sorry for that.

♥   ♥   ♥

I didn't call you back instantly after I saw you had cut the call. First because I thought you were still on another phone. And second because I didn't want to call you just to say nothing, while talking to other people and keeping you on hold. I didn't want to do that to you.

I know I make a lot of mistakes. But I want to be better for you. I want to know the way you want me to be. If you don't tell me why you're upset, how can I even fix myself? Please tell me. I am listening.

♥   ♥   ♥

I know you must be so tired of explaining things to me. And that breaks my heart. But I am trying. For you. For us. I know I am not always capable of treating you the way you deserve. But I will try my best, every single day. I never want to hurt you. I want to give you all the happiness you deserve. My intention is never, ever to hurt you.

And when I do mess up, please don't go silent on me. Please. Fight with me. Yell at me. Curse me if you have to. But please, please talk to me. I can handle your anger. I can take your frustration. But your silence? No. I cannot take it. It burns my heart when you stop talking to me. It truly does.

♥   ♥   ♥

Please forgive me. I will try to understand you more from now on.

With all the love I have, I love you, Mayalau. 💕 I love you soo much.
